## Installation  
<img align="left" src="{{ site.baseurl }}assets/images/blazing-fruits/icon.png" alt="icon" width="60" height="60"> Use Play Store to download 'Blazing Fruits' to your Android phone.  
Click [link](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.orana.game) for Google Play page.
  
## How to Play?
Tap your screen to burn the fruits whose color is matching with the background color.
